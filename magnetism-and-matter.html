📘 Theory Notes

Magnetic Dipole Moment: M = m × (2l)

Magnetic Field on Axis: B = (μ₀ / 4π) × (2M / r³)

Magnetic Field on Equatorial Line: B = (μ₀ / 4π) × (M / r³)

Bar Magnet Properties: North → South outside, South → North inside

⚡ Important Formulas

  • Torque: τ = M × B
  • Potential Energy: U = -M·B
  • Magnetization: M = magnetic moment / volume
  • Relation: B = μ₀ (H + M)
