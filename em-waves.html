Electromagnetic Waves (EM Waves)

🏠 Home Electric Charges and fields Current Electricity Magnetism and matter

📘 Theory

  • EM waves are produced by accelerating charges.
  • They do not need any medium → can travel in vacuum.
  • Speed of EM waves = c = 3 × 10⁸ m/s
  • Electric field (E) ⟂ Magnetic field (B) ⟂ Direction of wave
  • They are transverse waves.

⚡ Important Formulas

  • c = 1 / √(μ₀ε₀)
  • E = cB
  • Energy density = (ε₀E²)/2
  • Wave speed: v = fλ

🌈 EM Spectrum (Order)

Radio → Microwave → Infrared → Visible → UV → X-rays → Gamma rays

🧠 Tricks

  • Memory Trick: "Raging Martians Invaded Venus Using X-ray Guns"
  • Higher frequency → Higher energy
  • Gamma rays have maximum energy
