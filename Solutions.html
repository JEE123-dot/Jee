📘 Theory (JEE Level)

  • Solution: Homogeneous mixture of two or more components.
  • Molarity (M): moles of solute / volume (L)
  • Molality (m): moles of solute / mass (kg)
  • Henry’s Law: p = kH x → gas solubility
  • Raoult’s Law: p = p° x → ideal solutions
  • Ideal Solution: obeys Raoult law, ΔHmix = 0
  • Non-Ideal: +ve / -ve deviation
  • Azeotropes: constant boiling mixtures
  • Colligative Properties:
    • Relative lowering of vapour pressure
    • Elevation in boiling point
    • Depression in freezing point
    • Osmotic pressure

⚡ Important Formulas

  • ΔTb = Kb × m
  • ΔTf = Kf × m
  • π = CRT
  • i = observed / theoretical

💡 Tricks (High Yield)

  • 👉 Molality → Temperature independent
  • 👉 Molarity → Temperature dependent
  • 👉 More particles → More colligative effect
  • 👉 i > 1 → Dissociation, i < 1 → Association
  • 👉 Electrolytes give higher effect than non-electrolytes
